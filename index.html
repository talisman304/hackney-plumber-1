FAQ

Built for conversion: remove doubts and handle common objections.

What should I do if something is sparking or overheating?

If safe to do so, turn off power at the consumer unit and avoid using the circuit. If there’s burning smell, smoke, or visible damage, treat it as an emergency and call immediately.

Do you do EICR for landlords?

Yes. We can inspect, provide the report, and discuss any remedial work required to reach a satisfactory outcome.

Why does my RCD keep tripping?

Common causes include moisture ingress, a faulty appliance, insulation breakdown, or a wiring issue. Proper fault finding identifies the cause before replacing parts.

Can you install an EV charger at home?

Yes. We check supply capacity, cable routing, and installation requirements, then commission and test the unit.
